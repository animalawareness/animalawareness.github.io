About us

MISSION AND VISION

Animals are cruelly killed for entertainment purposes, the environment and the habitats of animals are destroyed, animals are poached for certain resources, and other cruel acts are committed by people. Unbeknownst to us all, as a selfish race, we have a negative impact on those around us and, although sharing the same planet, we are eradicating them by immoral means. Animal awareness is all about the animals that our species severely harmed and whose numbers were drastically reduced. This website is meant to educate visitors about how their activities can affect the environment and raise awareness of the need to strengthen animal protection measures.
